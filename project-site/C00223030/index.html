Pay As You Drive Motor Insurance App

This is an app that tracks driver trips and characteristics of driving to determine their insurance premiums.
Drivers could for example pay a flat rate to cover basic costs with an extra charge on top based on car usage
including area of use, time, speed, distance, etc. The driver pays for his/her trips on monthly basis. Car usage
tracking, monthly payment etc are managed through the app. A backend server provides access to configuring
the characteristics of driving considered in charging clients. Blockchain may be used as part of this implementation.
